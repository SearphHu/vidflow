Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use VidFlow, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address and username if you create an account.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the service, including prompts, generated content, and workflow configurations.
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device information, IP address, and cookies for analytics and service improvement.
  • Generated Content: The text prompts, images, and videos you create through the AI generation features.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining the VidFlow service
  • Processing AI generations through third-party API providers
  • Improving our algorithms and user experience
  • Communicating service updates and support
  • Ensuring platform security and preventing abuse

3. Third-Party Services

VidFlow integrates with third-party AI model providers to generate content. These providers receive your prompts and may process them according to their own privacy policies. We use the following types of services:

  • AI image generation models (Seedream, Image2)
  • AI video generation models (Seedance 2.0, Seedance Mini)
  • AI text generation models
  • Cloud infrastructure providers

4. Data Storage and Security

Your projects and workflow data are stored locally in your browser's local storage. Generated media files are stored temporarily on our servers for delivery. We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS) and access controls.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in your local projects
  • Delete your projects and local data at any time
  • Opt out of analytics cookies
  • Request information about data processing practices

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies for service functionality and optional analytics cookies for improving our service. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

VidFlow is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes through the service or via email.

9. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: support@vidflow.ai
